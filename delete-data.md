# Data Deletion Request — Bolt to Home

**App:** Bolt to Home
**Developer:** Brevabee Studios
**Last updated:** 2026-04-29

If you played **Bolt to Home** by **Brevabee Studios** and would like to request deletion of any data associated with your use of the Game, follow the steps below.

---

## What data exists

Bolt to Home does **not** ask for a name, email address, phone number, or any account sign-up. The vast majority of data in the Game is stored locally on your device and never leaves it.

### Stored only on your device (uninstall to delete)
- Current level reached and total levels unlocked
- Earned gems, star ratings, and high scores per level
- Sound and vibration preferences

To delete this data, simply **uninstall the Game** from your device. There is nothing to request from us — we never receive it.

### Collected by our advertising partner (Unity LevelPlay / IronSource)
The Game shows ads through the Unity LevelPlay SDK. The SDK collects:
- Your Google Advertising ID (GAID)
- Your IP address (used to derive approximate, city-level location)
- Ad interactions (impressions, clicks, completion events)
- App performance and crash diagnostics

This data is processed by Unity Technologies / Unity LevelPlay, not directly by Brevabee Studios.

---

## How to request deletion

### Step 1 — Email us
Send an email to **[brevabeestudios@gmail.com](mailto:brevabeestudios@gmail.com)** with:

- **Subject:** `Data Deletion Request — Bolt to Home`
- **Body:** A statement that you want your data deleted, and either:
  - Your **Google Advertising ID (GAID)** if you can provide it, or
  - The approximate date you started playing and your country of residence (we can pass this along to our advertising partner).

### Step 2 — We forward to our advertising partner
Within **7 business days** of receiving your email we will:
- Confirm receipt of your request
- Forward the request to Unity LevelPlay / IronSource using their data-subject-request channel
- Notify you when the request is acknowledged

### Step 3 — Reset advertising IDs on your own device (recommended)
You can also reset your advertising ID directly. This severs the link between your past ad-related activity and your future activity:

- **Android**: Settings → Google → Ads → **Delete advertising ID** (or **Reset advertising ID**)
- **iOS**: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → **Allow Apps to Request to Track** → toggle off; or Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → **Reset Advertising Identifier**

## What is deleted vs. kept

| Data | Deleted on request | Retained | Reason |
|------|--------------------|----------|--------|
| Local game progress (gems, levels, scores) | Self-served (uninstall the Game) | None | Stored only on your device |
| Your Google Advertising ID and ad-interaction history | Yes — within ~30 days of request | None | Forwarded to Unity LevelPlay for deletion |
| IP-derived approximate location | Yes — within ~30 days of request | None | Forwarded to Unity LevelPlay for deletion |
| Crash logs / diagnostics | Yes — within ~30 days of request | Aggregated, anonymized analytics may be retained for stability research | Aggregated data cannot be tied back to you |
| Your email address (if you contact us) | Yes — on request | Until the request is closed and for up to 30 days afterward for audit | Retained briefly so we can confirm completion of your request |

We retain **no other personal data** about you.
