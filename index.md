## 1. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, or phone number) from players. The Game stores your gameplay progress (level reached, gems, settings) only on your device.

However, our Game integrates third-party services that may automatically collect information about you and your device, including:

- **Device identifiers** — such as the Google Advertising ID (GAID) on Android, the Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) on iOS, and similar technical IDs.
- **Approximate location** — derived from your IP address (city or region level only, never precise GPS).
- **Device and network information** — operating system version, device model, language, time zone, network connection type, and screen resolution.
- **App activity and performance data** — sessions, crashes, performance metrics, and ad interactions (impressions, clicks).

This information is collected by our advertising and mediation partners listed in Section 4 below.

## 2. How We Use Information

The information collected is used to:

- Display advertisements within the Game (banner, interstitial, rewarded video).

## 3. Local Data on Your Device

The Game uses Unity's `PlayerPrefs` and local storage to persist:

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly contact us with a support request.
